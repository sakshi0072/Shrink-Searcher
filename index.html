We know times are tough.


One of the major reasons why people drop out of therapy or don’t start is guilt and fear of judgement.


By automating our system and only adding human interference when it is absolutely necessary, you can be assured that we’re not listening in on what you have to say.


Our state-of -the-art AI will match you with the perfect therapist for you as soon as you fill in the contact details and respond to a basic questionnaire that takes about 5-10 minutes to fill out.

Don’t like the therapist(s) you’ve been assigned?


Don’t fret. While we pride ourselves on having robust machine learning systems we don’t shy away from human communication.


If you like, we’ll have a volunteer ready to hear you out for the time being and connect you with the therapist you think is right for you.


Or you can simply send us an email after browsing through the profiles, and we’ll let you know your desired therapist’s schedule.

No need to go to a clinic.


Most of our experts offer online therapy with us. You can chat with them right on our website or our mobile app as well as keep track of your worksheets and progress.


You also have an option to send audio texts and video or voice call your therapist through the app.

Creating a Safe Community

We at Therafind are creating a safe and bias free community for all individuals. We aim at building a safe space that fosters support for people who go to therapy and reduce the stigma around mental health.

Affordable, Accessible Therapy

We partner with up & coming, remote therapists and bring their services online. This allows us to make therapy more accessible at affordable costs.

Find the right therapist for you

Our state of the art AI matches you with the perfect therapist for you based on your responses. You can be assured that you’re talking to somebody who gets you in a non judgemental environment.

Therapy from the comfort of your home

Through our app and website, your therapist is always there for you. You can text or call or send a voice note whenever you want. You can keep your therapist updated without ever having to leave your house or travel!
